# Dicey Dicey

A mobile-first split-screen dice game for two players on a single phone.

## How to Play

1. Place your phone flat on a table between two players
2. Each player sits on opposite sides — the top half is flipped for Player 2
3. Both players roll simultaneously by tapping their Roll button
4. After each roll, tap dice to freeze numbers you want to keep
5. After 3 rolls, the player with the most of a single number wins the round
6. Tiebreaker: higher number wins. Win the most rounds to win the game!

## Settings

- **Dice count**: 4–10 (default 6)
- **Rounds**: 1–9 (default 3)
- **Timer**: configurable countdown between games with selectable sounds
